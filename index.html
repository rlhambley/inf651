Welcome to the Spring Meadows Home Owner Association!

Located in Kenosha, WI near UW-Parkside,

the Spring Meadows Property Owners Association was founded in 1989. On April 28, 1989, the subdivision developers drafted the initial Restrictive Covenants for Spring Meadows Subdivision, with Section-V thereof reference Lots #1 through 27 as being in the Association. That document was recorded with the Kenosha County Register of Deeds on Page 360 of the Volumne 1348 as Document #819471. The Spring Meadows Subdivision consists of 130 Single Family homes and Condos.
